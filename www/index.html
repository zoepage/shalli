optimize for sanity

We make decisions all the time, many unconciously. Still, making good decisions is hard, and it's even harder in working contexts and Open Source Projects. Even when attempting to act professionally, think everything through and consider all implications, we are still humans: with our own individual needs, emotions and short attention spans. Sit down once, define your core values and what matters to you personally or as a community, and any time you're not sure if you want to accept a new inquiry from a client or implement a new feature in your Open Source project: just ask Shall I?. Shalli is the app that is there for you.
